About KushHub

Have lots of notes and don't know what to do it with them? No worries! Enter your note in our React.js form below. This form takes your title and content of your notes and sends a POST HTTP request to the KushHub database using Express.js. We securley store your notes on our MongoDB server. The KushHub App runs on a Node.JS runtime enviornment so our platform is highly scalable, secure, fast, and is easily maintained.
